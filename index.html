If you're here you're probably aware of what Uberil is but if you're not here's a quick overview. For those of you already aquainted please continue on your way to Homebrew above.


A Homebrew Adventure

Uberil is a hombrew setting developed by Joe Gillette (me) that alters the construction of locations, lore, and planes/dimensions in the D&D 5th Edition(5e) world. The changes are not to detract from D&D 5e content in any way shape or form, but rather to make things more cohesive or distinct for the player. The content provided on this website is completely free to use and transmit amongst personal groups and is not an officially licensed product (obviously), so use at your own risk or pleasure. My apologies for waffling on but I found it necessary to provide further legal seperation.

If you wish to post any requests for official changes or additions please contact rilvontechnologies@gmail.com, or for faster responses my personal email: gosarcreates@pm.me Good luck on your adventures traveler!
