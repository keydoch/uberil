Minor Portal - Measmus Cantrip

Cast Time: 1-3 Turns

Time Spent Casting Outcome
1 Turn Portals are 1 foot in diameter.
2 Turns Portals are 2 feet in diameter.
3 Turns Portals are 3 feet in diameter.

Range: The portal diameter determines the maximum range.

Portal Diameter Allowed Range
1 Foot 60 Feet
2 Feet 40 Feet
3 Feet 20 Feet

Components: Verbal, Somatic


Duration: Depends on time spent casting.

Time Spent Casting Spell Duration
1 Turn (6 seconds) 10 Turns (1 minute)
2 Turns (12 seconds) 20 Turns (2 minutes)
3 Turns (18 seconds) 30 Turns (3 minutes)
4 Turns (24 seconds) 40 Turns (4 minutes)
5 Turns (32 seconds) 50 Turns (5 minutes)

You open two shimmering portals that at the selected location; each humming with a strange power. All creatures within 10 feet of teh portal are alerted if they have a passive perception above 12. You can pass any object/spell/creature through these portals. You can use the two portals like a window, and can see through with ease though this is applied to those on the other side of the portals. You can send the portal through objects and walls beyond your scope of vision, though this does not mean that you are aware of what is on the other side.
