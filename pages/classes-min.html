Crafting (Level 1, 5, 10, 15, 20)

You are able to use recipes created by you and other blacksmiths by using the following process:

  1. Memorize Recipe DC: Int 10
  2. Smelt the applicable metals (See metal chart)
  3. Shape the metal into the specifications, using the DC check chart below.
    4. DexterityStrengthOutcome
    DC 10DC 10Common
    DC 12DC 10Rare

  4. Quench the item, maintaining proper timing; Int DC 10. Failure produces a warped weaopn that has a fixed -4 to all attacks or defense for shields and armor. Any products that fail during quenching will be destroyed after 30 uses.
  5. Add the hilt and handle.

Blacksmith's Concentration (Level 4)

You are a master of patience and concentration. Your concentration when producing an item is unable to be broken, allowing for better products. This causes a +4 to all checks relating to smithing


Hammer Time (Level 6)

You pull out your hammer, using your pent up rage against your opponents. How dare they insult your craftsment ship with their puny weapons?This causes a +4 to hit, +2 damage. You may also use your innate abilities to repair bent up weapons without a forge, reducing repair time.


Forge of the Heavens (Level 10)

You are now able to produce items with greater speed and fewer tools. You are now able to call down a forge and anvil that lasts for the duration of your concentration. If an enemy is hit with either the forge or anvil they will receive standard falling damage at a rate of 2d20. There is a low probability of being hit because the drop location is within a 20 foot radius, but is beyond your control. Your technique at lighting your forge has further developed, you are now able to light your forge with only simple friction. Your dexterity and strength stat increase by 3.


Rune Smithing (Level 15)

You are now able to imbue your weapons with the power of runes with attributes. There are 5 tiers of runes, each tier posessing 13 attributes. Each attribute determines the type of damage, or miscellaneous effect. Runes require a Dexterity check with a DC check of 15 to correctly carve, and 1 hour of dedicationfor T1, 2 hours for TII, 3 hours for TIII, 4 hours for TIV, 6 hours for TV. The effects do not stack. The maximum amount of runes allowed to be carved onto an item is 5.

Requirements for using this system is:

  1. A rune carving needle. (Available for purchase at blacksmith shops for 20gp.
  2. A nearby anvil to prop up your weapon.

Blacksmith's Breath (Level 17)

Your very breath enhances the items you produce by considerable margins. All items you make now have an additional 2 of their respective dice and a +12 chance to hit. Concentration must be in affect.


A Legend Forged (Level 20)

You are a forging legend, one to go down in history for his ability to create weapons and armor. You are given your own personal forge by your respective government. All items forged by your hand are now able to have an extra 2 runes engraved upon them, and your initial is magically transmitted upon all of them. The difficulty of all checks relating to smithing are lowered by 7, and damage from your weapons has one extra of their respective dice.

Class/Sub-Class: Merchant

You are a trader at heart and a master of coin, using your discerning eye to turn a profit.


Art of Trade (Level 1)

Your skill with trade allows you to purchase and sell at 10% increased revenue, and purchase at 10% decreased cost.


Merchant's Gold Card

Whenever a transaction is made your funds are magically deducted from your gold card (using your bank; function as a debet card). All companies recognize you as a VIP member.


Platinum Tongue (Level 1, 10, 20)

Your ability to convince others with promises of wealth is admirable. You are able to roll a Charisma check with a DC of 12 to effect a radius of 60 feet around you to do the following:

  • Boost your mercenary and ally morale, buffing their attack to hit by +4 at level 1, +6 at level 10, and +10 at level 20.
  • Reduce enemy morale, reducing their chance to hit by -4.

Merchant's Guards (Level 4)

You are able to hire up to 3 soldiers for 10 gold per 5 levels the soldier has. You are obligated to provide a minimum of lodging and equipment for the soldiers above level 5.


Company Standard (Level 8)

All companies under your name turn 2x greater profits through your excellent managment and advertising. Your company is a household name in your respective city, and you may speak with any local noble without appointment or fee.


Executive Treatment (Level 12)

You are recognized as a master of trade wherver you go, gaining 1,000 affinity throughout the mortal plane and 6,000 affinity throughout the ethereal market. You can now easily gain connections and the time to sell items is halved.


Private Bank (Level 16)

You may now own your own private bank. You may install further Banks for 1,000 platinum whenever you wish and recieve a fixed 250 platinum from investments per 2 weeks per bank, received on your Gold Card.


Tycoon Supreme (Level 20)

You, in honor of your achievements, are offered the position of Duke in 1 kingdom of your choice. In that kingdom you are excempted from taxes, increasing your revenue by 3x. You are given a jade slip granting free access to Leomund's Gate and 1 free stall at the Ethereal Market.
